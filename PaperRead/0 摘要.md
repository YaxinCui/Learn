跨语言目标词抽取旨在利用源语言中的知识去抽取目标语言的目标词。以往基于mBERT和XLM-R的方法在这个任务上实现了很好的效果。然而，一些研究指出由于预训练语言模型缺少句法、词法信息，另外不同语言之间存在共有语言信息。为了解决这个问题，我们考虑迁移Stanza中传统的语言独立的语言信息到XLM-R上。在这篇文章中，我们构造了一个GCN模块去迁移语言独立句法知识，构造了一个语言独立POS和NER嵌入想了，去迁移词法信息。我们在SemEval2016 Task5数据集上进行了实验，实验结果表明了我们的LDGL-XLMR能有效提高模型性能。

Cross-lingual aspect-based term extraction aims to utilize the knowledge in a source language to extract aspect terms in a target language. Prior methods based on mBERT and XLM-R have been applied to this task and achieved promising results. However, some studies point out that PTLMs lack linguistic information because of PLMs task-agnostic training method, and some linguistic information is universal in different languages.To solve it, we consider to transfer tradition language-dependent lexical and grammatical information to XLM-R with Stanza. In this paper, we constract a GCN module to transfer language-dependent grammatical knowledge, and constract a language-dependent POS and NER embedding to transfer lexical knowledge. We conduct experiments on SemEval2016 Task5 datasets and the experimental results show that our LDGL-XLMR significantly outperforms the state-of-the-art methods.
